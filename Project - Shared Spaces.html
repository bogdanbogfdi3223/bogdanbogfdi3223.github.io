PS1 - Project Shared Spaces

For the subject of PS1, we learned how to do basic research, as well understand the approach of research a subject and developing the skills required for that.
Part of that research understanding concerns user trialling.
In user trialling, we practiced the research technique of observing a user trying out a product after which we can get knowledge about the user experience of the product.
The aim of this research was to get insight into the user experience of the website of "Rijksstudio" by observing a participant that we chose ourselves.
At the end of the project, we achieved the goal of expanding our research skills and knowledge as an outcome, which will surely help us in future situations.

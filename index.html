Who is Bogdan

About Me

My name is Bogdan and I am a third year student at the University of Applied Sciences in The Hague, studying the User Experience Design programme.

Aside my passion for working in Adobe XD, Photoshop and creating Pixel art, I am also passionate in customer service, therefore spending some of my time outside school as a Shift Leader/Managaer at a mexican themed restaurant. Through my experience as a manager that deals with tasks such as data administration, orders, training people and taking care of the restaurant I am assigned to work at, I aim that once I receive my UXD degree, I can follow the path of becoming a manager that would take ownership over newly created projects and lead new UX Designers to a career full of knowledge and abundance.

After living for 19 years in Romania, I decided that is it time to seek more opportunities for my future by moving to the Netherlands. I am soon to celebrate my fourth year here and by how things have gone so far, without any doubt I can say that the lifestyle of this country suits everything that I had hoped for, especially since there are a lot of opportunities for any upcoming UX designers, like me.

Outside of school and work, I enjoy going out with my friends, as well as watch animated-series and play video-games on my PC/phone, plus review a bunch of apps on Google Play Store with my (so far) gained knowledge in terms of UX/UI Deisgn in hope the companies would take my feedback into account and provide a better experience for the apps/games I use on my daily life.

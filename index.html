About Me

My name is Nicolas-Bogdan and I am a second year student at the University of Applied Sciences in The Hague, studying the User Experience Design programme.

Recently I ended my career at the Burgers and Fries restaurant, Five Guys (in Utrecht), where I have been working as a shift manager for more than 3 quarters of my time there.

After living for 19 years in Romania, I decided that is it time to seek more opportunities for my future by moving to the Netherlands. I am soon to celebrate my third year here and by how things have gone so far, without any doubt I can say that the lifestyle of this country suits everything that I had hoped for.

Outside of school and work, I enjoy going out with my friends, as well as watch animated-series and play video-games on my PC/phone.

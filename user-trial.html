User Trial - Rijksstudio

For the subject of Research for Design, we get to learn how to do basic research. We need to build an understanding of research and develop research skills.
Part of that research understanding concerns user trialling.
In user trialling, we will be practicing the research technique of observing a user trying out a product after which we can get knowledge about the user experience of the product.
The aim of this research was to get insight into the user experience of the website of "Rijksstudio" by observing a participant that we chose ourselves.
We will expand our research skills and knowledge as an outcome, which will surely help us in future situations.
